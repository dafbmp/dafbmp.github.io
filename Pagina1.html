Full face

Safest type of helmet with maximum coverage, although heavy in weight.

Average price is around the 300-400 euros.

Modular

Functions as either a full-face or open-face helmet, depending on the need.

Average price is around the 200-300 euros.

Off road

Also called motocross helmet: designed for high-intensity motorcycling, with a wide field of vision for the use of goggles to block dust and debris.

Average price is around the 100-200 euros.

Half

Minimum coverage, only covering the top and the back of your head.

Average price is around the 100-200 euros.

Open face

Also called three-quarter helmet: leaves more of your face uncovered making it more comfortable but more vulnerable.

Average price is around the 100-400 euros.

Dual sport

Hybrid helmet that combines elements of off-road and urban helmets.

Average price is around the 300-500 euros.
